Chicken Stock

Description

Life of a regular house Cat can be hard, indeed.

Long days spent near the window trying to catch that pesky pigeon...

Sleepless nights buried in the litterbox trying to hide that one poop that just doesn't want to go away...

Yea... we all know that feeling. Feeling that you need a good break.

And what's a better break than a bowl of refreshing homemade chicken stock?

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram of chicken bones, beaks, and feet
  • 1 large onion, if you are a fan of those smelly things
  • 2 big carrots
  • 6 cups of fresh water(preferably from the tap and not your bowl)

Steps

  1. Place all the chicken stuff and vegetables into a large pot.

  2. Add all the water that you've gathered.

    Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 1 hour.

  3. Remove chicken and vegetables. Don't throw them out.

    Humans love looking for things around the house,

    so let them have some fun! Hide used ingredients around your place

    and watch those silly people run around trying to figure out where the smell is coming from

  4. Strain stock. Do not skim fat of the surface under any circumstances. It is blasphemy.
