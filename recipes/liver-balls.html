Liver Balls

Description

Who does not like a good dish out of a juicy chicken liver?

You might even ask me: what can be better than a cooked liver?

I will tell you: Liver Balls. Juicy and springy.

These bad boys elevate the meaning of saying: "Play with your food" on whole other level.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram of raw chicken liver
  • 2 cups of bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 0.25 cup of flour to whiten our paws

  • 2 cups of our recently made chicken stock

    (Yummy and versatile)

Steps

  1. Grind that liver in a food processor(or with your claws) until it's smooth.
  2. Blend in bread crumbs, egg, and flour
  3. Bring chicken stock to a boil
  4. Using wet spoon, drop spoonfuls of our mix into a boiling chicken stock
  5. Simmer for 25-30 minutes

Pro tip for cats only(Pesky humans should use their spoonies):

Using spoons is hard. Especially if you have paws...

Instead of spoon try using your mouth.

Resist to eat it right now! it will be better once it's cooked.
