Despription

Evenings... that sorrowful time of the day.

Chicken broth is all gone.

And it only took you an hour to hunt down all liver balls...

Even the emergency bowl of dry food is empty...

Alas, The Hunger, hunger is almost omnipresent

Humans can't even comprehend what an immence amount of concentration is required...

too keep that Black Hole that is our stomach at bay.

But do not fret fellow feline.

I know The Way to supress it atleast till morning.

And that way is called: Ukha or common fish soup.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 onion, chipped(We've met again my eternal enemy)
  • 1 bunch of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 kilogram of cod fillets, cubed.(Resist to eat it for now).
